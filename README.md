Welcome to the WMR community! We are a herd of builders, innovators, and thinkers. Our development is fueled by collective intelligence, and our growth is sustained by transparency.

Community Governance
This project is not directed by a single entity; it is steered by the community through our primary communication channels on X.com and Reddit. We invite you to join the conversation, propose features, and help us refine our roadmap.

The Annual Gathering
To honor our roots and align our vision, the WMR community holds an Annual State of the Herd meeting every January 3rd. This is our moment to reflect on the past year, discuss the evolution of our code, and set the trajectory for the months ahead. All contributors are welcome to join.

The Trinity of Systems: Honor & Management
We recognize the pillars that have shaped computing history. In honor of Microsoft, Linux, and iOS, we have organized our management, payment structures, and benefits distribution into three distinct functional guilds:

The Microsoft Guild (Enterprise & Integration): Focused on corporate stability, legacy system compatibility, and professional-grade tooling. Management of professional benefit packages and large-scale corporate contributions occurs here.

The Linux Guild (Core & Reciprocity): The heart of our "Herd" philosophy. This guild oversees the open-source core, kernel optimizations, and ensuring that all contributions adhere to the Collaborative Herd License (CHL). This is where our primary collective bounty systems are managed.

The iOS Guild (Experience & Interface): Focused on accessibility, design, user experience, and mobile-first logic. This guild manages the creative benefits, distribution of "ANVERSO" digital assets, and community recognition awards.

How to Get Involved
Engage: Participate in discussions on [X.com] and [Reddit].

Propose: If you have an idea, draft a proposal and share it with the relevant Guild.

Collaborate: Pull requests are the lifeblood of WMR. Ensure your code complies with the CHL and follow the established formatting conventions.

Manage: Join one of the three guilds above to participate in the oversight of project funds and contributor benefits.

Remember: We are advancing in a herd. Your contributions today form the foundation of our shared prosperity for the next 10 years.
